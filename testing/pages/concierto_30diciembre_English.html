There are not words to express what happened last 30 December. Yes, I achieved it. Some years ago, I had the illusion to have an afternoon with music, percussion, which I love, surrounded by my people. For some time, the main idea to approach the wide world of percussion to people from Cantalejo and the region emerged. In the concert I wanted to show a great variety of styles, instruments, possibilities and musical repertories for all audiences with original plays to percussion, adaptations and plays to "the other percussion". Hence, I mixed up music and other disciplines that I constantly follow. The result could not be better; all together, relatives, family and friends, enjoyed a special afternoon plenty of Music and great feelings. Thank you very much for making of that day one of the most unbelievable, I won't forget it! This is the reason why I do it!

Detmold, 10 January, 2018

Thank you very much to all who spent their time in the 30th December concert!

I am so grateful!

Concert 30 December 2017
